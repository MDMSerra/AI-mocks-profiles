🔒 Privacy and Data Protection

At CapybAI, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your data. Below are our revised principles and guidelines:

🛡️ Core Principles

  • Transparency: We clearly outline how your data is collected, used, and protected.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the necessary data to enhance user experience.
  • Enhanced Anonymity: User interactions are anonymized, and identifiable information is stripped where possible.
  • Stronger Security: Data is encrypted with industry-leading standards and access is restricted to essential personnel.
  • User Rights: Users can access, modify, or request deletion of their data at any time.

📊 Data We Collect

Type of Data Purpose Retention Period
Interaction Metrics Improve AI recommendations 6 months
Usage Statistics Enhance platform performance 3 months
Anonymous Feedback Identify user needs and issues Until addressed
Diagnostic Logs Troubleshooting and security 60 days
Account Preferences Personalize user experience Until account deletion

Note: No personally identifiable information (e.g., name, address) is collected unless explicitly provided by the user.

🗂️ How We Use Your Data

  1. Service Enhancement: Improve platform functionality and AI accuracy.
  2. Research & Development: Use anonymized data to refine AI predictions.
  3. Security & Fraud Prevention: Monitor for potential threats and unauthorized access.
  4. Testing & Optimization: Evaluate new features for stability and usability.
  5. User Personalization: Adapt AI interactions based on user preferences.

🤝 User Responsibilities

By using CapybAI, users agree to:

  1. Provide accurate and truthful data where necessary.
  2. Use the platform in accordance with its terms and policies.
  3. Recognize that AI-generated recommendations should be independently verified.
  4. Stay informed about privacy updates and policy changes.

💡 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can I request my data to be deleted?

Yes, you can request data deletion at any time by contacting privacy@capybai.com.

2. How is my data anonymized?

We remove all personal identifiers and only retain general usage patterns.

3. Does CapybAI share data with third parties?

No, we do not sell or share user data. It is used solely for platform improvement.

4. What happens in case of a data breach?

Affected users will be notified within 48 hours, and corrective measures will be taken immediately.

5. Can I opt out of data collection?

Yes, you can adjust your privacy settings in your account or contact us to limit data collection.

For further inquiries or privacy concerns, please reach out to privacy@capybai.com.
